Please visit these websites for more information on COVID-19:

- CDC.gov

Centers for Disease Control and Prevention or CDC is probably the biggest source of information related to COVID-19 or any disease. You will most likely find the most information here.

- NIH.gov

National Institute of Health or NIH is a source that has articles which contain general information on the coronavirus and its impact. The articles are written by the medical research agency.

- Cases Update

Worldometers is a source where you can view how many coronavirus cases, deaths, and recoveries there are. It also includes daily cases, daily deaths, closed cases, and active cases.

- COVID-19 Census

The Census COVID-19 Data Hub has the demographic and economic resources analysis. It also contains new Covid-19 surveys and estimates which are innovative statistical products.

- COVID-19 Map

This source contains a map which shows how many COVID-19 cases are in each country. It shows a list of the countries from the most to least cases along with global deaths and recoveries.

- OSHA.gov

This database includes an overview, news and updates, hazard recognition, standards, medical information, control and prevention, and additional resources all relating to the coronavirus.
