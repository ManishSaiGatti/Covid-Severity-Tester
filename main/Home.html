What is COVID-19?
SARS-CoV-2 or more commonly known as COVID-19 or the coronavirus is a virus that emerged in China late 2019. COVID causes many symptoms, some of which are breathing difficulties, diarrhea, fatigue, and many more. COVID-19 can be very severe and in some cases cause death. COVID-19 can be spread from one person to another. There is no cure or vaccine for COVID-19 right now, so the best way to prevent it is to frequently wash your hands, maintain at least 6 feet of distance from other people, and wear a cloth face covering when going outside. If you are sick, make sure you stay inside.
What does our tool do?
Research shows that COVID itself has 6 different levels, each varying in symptoms and severity. This tool allows you to choose the symptoms you have, and tells you the severity of your case.
Note:
Use caution when using this tool. Do not use this website as a substitute for proper testing, as it is not 100% accurate. If you are ever feel that your symptoms are worsening, no matter the results of the test, immediately see a doctor. Again, this test is not 100% accurate.
